About Us

The Socratic Exchange exists to elevate discourse in business and economics through candid, long-form conversations with scholars and industry leaders.

We share stories, challenges, and ideas—offering insight that goes beyond headlines.

Join a community committed to rigor, ethics, and real-world impact.

Our Educational Mission

The Socratic Exchange is a cutting edge incubator and seminar grounded in the foundational ideas of economic and philosophical thinkers. From the moral sentiments of Adam Smith to Malthus’ theory on the limits to economic growth, our seminars aim to bridge nuanced business theories and real-world applications. By engaging students with professors and entrepreneurs from Stanford, Berkeley, USC, and Cal Haas, we drive students to develop their critical thinking skills towards meaningful innovations—all in 6 weeks.

Course Highlights:

  • Core Economic Principles: From macro to micro.
  • Entrepreneurial Strategy: Market analysis and validation.
  • Leadership & Ethics: Building sustainable and responsible ventures.
